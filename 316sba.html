Get Involved

Emergency Shelter: Our Emergency Shelter provides a safe and supportive environment for individuals and families in immediate crisis. We offer temporary housing, basic necessities, and case management services to help residents regain stability.

Counseling Services: Our professional counseling services offer emotional and psychological support for those coping with trauma, stress, or mental health challenges.

Legal Assistance: The Legal Assistance program provides guidance for those facing legal challenges, especially in cases involving family law, eviction prevention, and protective orders.

Support Groups: Our support groups bring together individuals who share similar life experiences, offering a safe space to connect and find strength in community.

Financial Literacy Workshops: These workshops are designed to empower individuals with the skills needed for financial independence.

Advocacy and Outreach: Our Advocacy and Outreach program raises awareness about issues affecting our community, such as domestic violence and homelessness.
